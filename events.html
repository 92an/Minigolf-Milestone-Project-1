Seniors Day Event


The first Saturday every month we arrange a senior play event. Discounts on snacks for those over 65. This event is organized and promoted by the senior citizen organization to encourage the elderly to partake in an active lifestyle. This is also a great opportunity to bond with grandchildren.

National tournaments


In Sweden there are various national tournaments arranged in different counties with varying formats. If you are interested in playing at a more professional level you can see what tournaments are currently active in Sweden at the miniature golf federation.
