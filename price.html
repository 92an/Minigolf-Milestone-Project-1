Membership Benefits


The benefit of membership is that you get to golf anytime you want for an entire year. Additionally, you get a discount of 10% in the store, and up to 20% on selected items. You will get sent newsletter about special events to your email and you get to participate in our members tournament. Further you will have a say in how the club operates in club meetings and participate in the community.
